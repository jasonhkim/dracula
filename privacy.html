DRACULA

Privacy Policy · Effective 13 July 2026

The short version

Dracula does not collect your data. No accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking. We never see who you are or where you go.

Location

Your location is used only on your device to center the map, compute shade routes, and guide you while navigating. To calculate routes and fetch local weather, your coordinates are sent transiently to open public services — OSRM and Overpass (OpenStreetMap) for routing and building data, Open-Meteo for weather and UV, and Nominatim/Photon for address search. These requests are processed in real time and are not linked to your identity by us. We do not store, log, or share your location.

Search history

Recent destinations are saved only on your device so they can be suggested next time. They never leave your phone, and deleting the app deletes them.

Third-party services

Map tiles are served by CARTO using OpenStreetMap data. Each service processes requests under its own privacy policy; Dracula sends them only what is technically necessary (map coordinates) and nothing about you.

Children

Dracula does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the new version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
